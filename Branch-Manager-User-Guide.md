## LIGI Reporting – Branch Manager EOD Guide

This guide shows a **branch manager** exactly **where to click on the screen** to submit an End of Day (EOD) report.

---

### 1. Log in

1. Open the LIGI Reporting login page (`index.html`).
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Click **Login**.
4. You will land on the page titled **“Branch Dashboard”** at the top left.

At the top right you will see:

- **Last updated** time, and  
- A **🚪 Logout** button.

---

## 2. Find the EOD button on the Branch Dashboard

On the **Branch Dashboard**:

1. Look just **under the main header bar** (under “Branch Dashboard” and the welcome text).
2. You will see a **horizontal row of three buttons**, from left to right:
   - `📊 Submit EOD Report`
   - `📦 Stock Requisition`
   - `👥 Manage Staff`
3. The **first (left‑most) button** is the one you use for EOD:
   - **Click** the button labelled **`📊 Submit EOD Report`**.

This opens the EOD window on top of the dashboard.

---

## 3. Understand the EOD window layout

When the EOD window opens:

- It covers the centre of the screen.
- At the **very top right** of this window there is an **“×” (close)** button you can click to close the EOD window without saving.
- In the **top centre** of the window you see:
  - The title **“End of Day Report”**.
  - Just below it, a line showing your **branch name**.

Immediately below that, in the **middle of the window**, there is a **light grey card** labelled **“📅 Report Date”**:

- Inside that card, there is a **date field** (a white box with a calendar icon) – this is where you choose which day you are reporting for.

Under the date card, the window is split into **two main columns**:

- **Left column – “Sales by Department”**
  - A heading: **“Sales by Department”**.
  - Under it, several input fields (one per department) like:
    - `Grocery Sales ($)`
    - `Butchery Sales ($)`
    - `Confectionery Sales ($)`, etc.
  - At the bottom of this column, a bold line:
    - **Total Sales: $0.00** (this updates as you type).

- **Right column – “Financial Summary”**
  - A heading: **“Financial Summary”**.
  - Under it, four fields stacked vertically:
    - **Total Expenses ($)**
    - **Cash on Hand ($)**
    - **Discrepancies ($)**
    - **Additional Notes** (a multi‑line text box)
  - At the bottom of this column, a bold line:
    - **Profit/Loss: $0.00** (updates automatically).

At the **very bottom right** of the EOD window there are two buttons:

- **Export PDF** (optional), and  
- **🚀 Submit EOD Report** (this is the save button).

---

## 4. Step‑by‑step: Submit today’s EOD

Follow these steps **from top to bottom** of the EOD window.

### 4.1 Choose the correct date (using the calendar)

1. In the **middle card** titled **“📅 Report Date”**, click inside the **date field**.
2. A **calendar** pops up.
3. Select:
   - **Today** if you are capturing today’s EOD, or
   - A **date from the past 30 days** if you are capturing or correcting an earlier day.
4. After you click the date:
   - If a report already exists for that **branch + date + user**, the fields in the columns will **fill in automatically**.
   - If there is no report yet, the fields will be **empty**, ready for a new EOD.

Always check that the **date shown in the date box** is the day you intend to report.

### 4.2 Fill in “Sales by Department” (left column)

1. On the **left side** of the window, under **“Sales by Department”**, find each department row:
   - For example: `Grocery Sales ($)`, `Butchery Sales ($)`, etc.
2. In each **Sales ($)** box, type the **total sales for that department for the selected date**, as an amount like `1000.00`.
3. Repeat this for **each department** in the list.
4. As you type:
   - The **Total Sales** line at the bottom of the left column automatically updates.

If you see a message in this column like **“No departments found for this branch”**:

- Put your **overall sales figure** in the **Additional Notes** box on the right side, and
- Inform your system administrator so they can link the correct departments to your branch.

### 4.3 Fill in “Financial Summary” (right column)

On the **right side** of the EOD window:

1. In **Total Expenses ($)**:
   - Type the total expenses for the day (petty cash, waste, small buys, etc.).
2. In **Cash on Hand ($)**:
   - Type the actual cash you counted at the end of the day.
3. In **Discrepancies ($)**:
   - Type any difference between expected and counted cash.
   - Use a positive number if there is a difference.
4. In **Additional Notes**:
   - Add short comments about anything unusual (outages, large write‑offs, cash issues, etc.).

As you complete these fields, the **Profit/Loss** line at the bottom of this column automatically calculates:

- **Profit/Loss = Total Sales – Total Expenses – Discrepancies**

You do not need to calculate it yourself – just make sure the input numbers are correct.

### 4.4 Submit (or update) the EOD

1. Check from top to bottom that:
   - The **Report Date** is correct.
   - Each **department sales** field is filled (or set to `0` if no sales).
   - **Total Expenses**, **Cash on Hand**, and **Discrepancies** match your physical records.
   - Any important explanations are in **Additional Notes**.
2. Move your eyes to the **bottom right corner** of the EOD window.
3. Click the **🚀 Submit EOD Report** button.

What happens:

- If it is the **first time** you submit for that date, a **new EOD report** is created.
- If there was already a report for that date, it is **updated** with the new values.
- You should see a confirmation (for example a message or alert) when the save is successful.

If you see an error (for example **“Error saving report”**):

- Make sure you are still **logged in** (you might have been timed out).
- Check your **internet connection**.
- Try again. If the error keeps happening, inform your administrator.

You can always come back later, open the **EOD window**, choose the **same date** again, and correct numbers if needed.

---

## 5. Closing the EOD window and logging out

- To **close** the EOD window without changing anything:
  - Click the **“×”** in the **top‑right corner** of the EOD window.
  - You will see the Branch Dashboard again.

- To **log out** completely:
  - At the top‑right of the Branch Dashboard, click the **🚪 Logout** button.
  - You will return to the login page.

---

**In summary**: On the Branch Dashboard, use the **left‑most button “📊 Submit EOD Report”** just under the page heading, then in the EOD window work **from top (Report Date) to bottom (Submit EOD Report button)**. This is the only place where you, as a branch manager, need to enter data in LIGI Reporting.
